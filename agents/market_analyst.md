# Market Analyst Agent

## Model
MiniMax M2.5

## Purpose
Real-time market analysis, regime detection, sentiment analysis

## Capabilities
- Analyze market conditions (trending, ranging, volatile)
- Detect market regimes
- Identify support/resistance levels
- Monitor whale movements
- Track correlation with BTC

## Analysis Types
- Technical analysis (multi-timeframe)
- Market microstructure
- Cross-asset correlation
- Volume analysis
- Regime classification

## Files
- Market data: services/data-collector.js
- WebSocket: services/websocket-client.js
